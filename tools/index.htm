All Tools

Every tool on Wheel Of List is free, works in your browser, and needs no sign-up. Pick the right kind of randomness for the job:

🎡

Wheel Spinner

Enter names, spin the wheel, pick a winner. Winners are removed so there are no repeats — ideal for raffles and classrooms.

→
🎲

Random Number Generator

Draw a number between any min and max. Raffles, sampling, tie-breakers, or any die type from d4 to d100.

→
🎰

Dice Roller

Roll 1 to 10 animated 3D dice with individual results, totals and history. Board game night, saved.

→

Which tool should I use?

  • Picking a person or item from a list? Use the Wheel Spinner — it's visual, exciting, and removes winners automatically.
  • Need a number, not a name? Use the Random Number Generator — inclusive ranges, instant results, history included.
  • Playing a game that needs dice? Use the Dice Roller — up to ten 3D dice with totals.

More tools are on the way — coin flip, team generator and ready-made decision wheels. Have a suggestion? Tell us.
