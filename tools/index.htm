Which tool should I use?

  • Picking a person or item from a list? Use the Wheel Spinner — it's visual, exciting, and removes winners automatically.
  • Deciding between exactly two options? Flip a coin — or spin the Yes or No Wheel for extra suspense.
  • Need a number, not a name? Use the Random Number Generator — inclusive ranges, instant results, history included.
  • Playing a game that needs dice? Use the Dice Roller — up to ten 3D dice with totals.
  • Splitting a group into teams? The Team Generator deals everyone into fair random sides.
  • Drawing giveaway winners? The Raffle Picker draws N winners in ranked order, no repeats.
  • Playing word games? The Random Letter Generator keeps Stop and Scattergories honest.

Every tool is free, runs entirely in your browser, and your data never leaves your device. Have a suggestion? Tell us.
